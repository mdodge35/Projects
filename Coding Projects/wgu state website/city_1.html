Pensacola

Pensacola is a city located on the coastline of Florida. It was first settled in 1559 and then resettled
in 1667. The city is mainly known for its white sands and clear blue water. Pensacola gets its main source
of income from tourism. The historic city caters to all interests especially those into music, history, and art.
Truly a must see destination in the sunshine state.
