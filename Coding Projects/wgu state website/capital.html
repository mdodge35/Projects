Tallahassee

Tallahassee is the capitol of Florida. Home to Gov. Ron Desantis and FSU's Seminoles. Tallahassee is not only the heart of Florida but a bustling college town with plenty to offer. Some notable individuals who have lived here are Jim Morrison, T-Pain and Kent Jones. From museums and natural springs to football games and sporting events. This two time All American City award winning city has something for everyone.
