Orlando

Orlando,as you may already know, is known for its theme parks. Home to ever growing Disney World and the magic of Harry Potter World in Universal Studios. Orlando was settled in 1843 and the town was incorporated in 1875. The Current mayo is Buddy Dyer and the main source of income for Orlando is tourism. Orlando is very family oriented, and while a little more on the expensive side, a trip worth saving for.
